How the program works

When a friend uses your unique code or link to make their first OneSkin purchase, $15 is automatically deducted at their checkout, and a $15 credit is added to your next shipment within 24 hours of their order shipping.

Eligibility & restrictions

Referral rewards are available on first-time orders over $40, on the OS-01 product line. They cannot be combined with other discounts or applied to gift cards. The referral program is available in the United States, Canada, and the EU.

How to redeem your credit

Credit is automatically applied to your next subscription renewal. If you have no active subscription, your credit is held in your account and applied to your next one-time order at checkout.
