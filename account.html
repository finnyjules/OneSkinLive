Frequently asked questions

What is your returns policy?

We offer a 60-day satisfaction guarantee on all orders. If OS-01 isn’t right for you, contact us within 60 days of delivery for a full refund — no return shipment required.

When will my order ship?

Most orders ship within 1–2 business days, with delivery typically arriving in 3–5 business days after that. Once your order ships you’ll get a tracking email, and you can follow it from this page under Arriving soon.

What do I do if I didn’t receive my order?

First, check the tracking link in your shipment email — packages sometimes arrive a day after they’re marked delivered. If it’s still missing after that, email us at help@oneskin.co with your order number and we’ll resend or refund.

How do I manage my subscription?

Open Manage subscription from your account home to change frequency, swap products, skip a shipment, or pause anytime. Changes take effect on your next scheduled shipment.
