Frequently asked questions

What makes OS-01 BODY Moisturizer different from a standard body lotion?

OS-01 BODY Moisturizer is the first body moisturizer built on longevity science. Unlike a standard lotion that hydrates the surface, it’s powered by the proprietary OS-01 Peptide™, engineered to address a key driver of visible skin aging at the cellular level.¹ Because skin is your largest organ, OS-01 BODY Moisturizer addresses the root causes of crepey, thinning skin while supporting whole-body longevity. It’s much more than a regular moisturizer.

When will I see results from OS-01 BODY Moisturizer?

Most customers notice immediate softness and hydration from the first use. By week 4, clinical study participants reported visibly smoother, more hydrated skin. By week 12, participants showed up to a +41% increase in skin barrier strength along with a measurable reduction in systemic inflammation markers.¹,³

Is OS-01 BODY Moisturizer suitable for sensitive skin?

Yes. OS-01 BODY Moisturizer is non-comedogenic, Dermatologically Tested for Sensitive Skin (n=54), and has been awarded the National Eczema Association Seal of Acceptance™. The formula is suitable for sensitive and reactive skin types.

Can I use OS-01 BODY Moisturizer on my face?

OS-01 BODY Moisturizer was formulated specifically for body skin, with active ingredients and nourishing oils suited to thicker skin. For facial skin, OS-01 FACE Moisturizer or OS-01 EYE Cream is more suitable. That said, some customers in drier climates or with drier skin have reported using OS-01 BODY Moisturizer on their face as well.

How do I use OS-01 BODY Moisturizer?

Apply OS-01 BODY Moisturizer to your entire body, or focus on specific areas where you want to see visible firming and smoothing. For best results, use daily — morning or evening.
