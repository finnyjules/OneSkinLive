Frequently asked questions

How long will my OS-01 FACE last?

When used twice daily as directed, one bottle should last approximately 1–2 months.

Are products safe while pregnant/breastfeeding?

OneSkin products are formulated without sulfates, parabens, or phthalates, and the ingredients have not been shown to be harmful during pregnancy or breastfeeding. However, because our products have not been specifically tested in pregnant or breastfeeding individuals, we recommend consulting your healthcare provider before introducing any new products during this time. For more information on the safety of the OS-01 Peptide™, click here.

How does OS-01 FACE compare to retinol?

Retinol and the OS-01 Peptide™ can lead to similar visible improvements, but they work in fundamentally different ways and can result in different skin responses. Retinol is a small molecule that increases cellular turnover by accelerating the shedding of the skin’s top layer. While it supports collagen and cell turnover, it does not address the aged, dysfunctional cells that drive skin aging, and it can cause irritation and increase sun sensitivity. The OS-01 Peptide™ is designed to support overall skin health in a gentle, non-irritating way by helping healthy, functional cells thrive. The result is skin that looks and feels healthier and younger over time.

What is OS-01 and what does it do?

OS-01 is a peptide engineered by our longevity scientists to reverse the visible signs of senescence, including collagen loss, skin thinning, and barrier decline. Over time, the OS-01 Peptide™ supports skin that looks and feels younger and healthier, and is generally more resilient to environmental factors and aging.
